# Alternative Name Suggestions (Non-Compound Words)

**Date**: 2025-11-12
**Purpose**: Creative alternatives to compound word names for Academic Explorer rebrand

## Overview

This document presents name suggestions that avoid simple compound words (like "ScholarWeave" or "CitationMesh"). These alternatives use:
- Invented/portmanteau words
- Latin/Greek-derived terms
- Shortened/modified forms
- Single evocative words
- Brandable neologisms

---

## Category 1: Invented Words / Neologisms

### Scholarly + Elegant

**1. Scholara**
- **Origin**: Scholar + aura/aurora
- **Pronunciation**: skol-AH-rah
- **Meaning**: Evokes scholarly atmosphere, intellectual illumination
- **Vibe**: Elegant, memorable, rolls off the tongue
- **Domain Status**: .com listed as "ready for development" (likely parked/for sale)

**2. Akademos**
- **Origin**: Greek akademos (garden where Plato taught)
- **Pronunciation**: ak-ah-DAY-mos
- **Meaning**: The original academy, birthplace of learning
- **Vibe**: Classical, authoritative, rooted in history
- **Note**: akademos.com appears available

**3. Erudia**
- **Origin**: Erudite + India/media
- **Pronunciation**: eh-ROO-dee-ah
- **Meaning**: Land of the learned
- **Vibe**: Sophisticated, fluid, memorable

**4. Scholium**
- **Origin**: Latin scholium (marginal note, commentary)
- **Pronunciation**: SKO-lee-um
- **Meaning**: Scholarly annotation or explanation
- **Vibe**: Academic, classical, precise
- **Note**: CONFLICT - scholium.ai exists (AI research assistant)

**5. Epistema**
- **Origin**: Greek episteme (knowledge, understanding)
- **Pronunciation**: ep-ih-STEE-mah
- **Meaning**: Knowledge, scientific understanding
- **Vibe**: Intellectual, Greek philosophy roots
- **Domain**: epistemia.com likely available (check variation)
